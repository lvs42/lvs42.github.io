Escape Room in Virtual Reality to prevent cognitive impairment

As my Bachelor's Thesis I developed a virtual reality escape room designed as a preventive tool against cognitive decline. I used Unreal Engine, employing Blueprints and C++, to implement the game logic, design interactive puzzle mechanics, and program the entire experience.

Cognitive decline is an increasingly significant issue in our society due to rising life expectancy, which leads to more cases of people affected by this condition. Characterized by a progressive decline in mental abilities such as memory, attention, and problem-solving, cognitive deterioration affects the quality of life of those who suffer from it. In this context, preventive strategies have become an area of great interest, with particular focus on the use of innovative technologies.
This project explores the development of a escape room using virtual reality technology as a preventive tool. By combining virtual reality and gamification, the project aims to create engaging challenges designed to stimulate users' cognitive abilities. This project achieves a natural interaction with elements in the virtual environment thanks to employing technologies for hand tracking and a realistic object-grasping system.
Additionally, a tracking system was implemented to monitor player performance, storing in-game metrics that can later be analyzed by professionals. This feature enhances the project's potential as an assessment tool, allowing for a more personalized approach to cognitive health monitoring.


Achievments

  • Creation of a Puzzle System: Development of a modular system that allows customization of puzzles and streamlines their creation.
  • Puzzle Implementation: Design and programming of interactive puzzles aimed at stimulating cognitive skills (addressing attention, comprehension and perception, logical reasoning, memory, and executive control, among others). The puzzles include different difficulty levels based on the user's abilities.
  • Analysis & Tracking: Design of a data storage architecture in JSON format to record player performance (puzzle resolution time, object interaction, trajectory).
  • Gamification: Application of gamification techniques to enhance user motivation and engagement.
  • Mechanics Programming: Implementation of interactive mechanics.
  • Performance Optimization: Implementation of interactive mechanics.
  • 3D Modeling and Design: Optimization to ensure a smooth experience without compromising graphic quality.
